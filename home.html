OUR HISTORY

Arise African Child Foundation was founded in the year 2015 on 1st of October. Two young visionaries while students at Maseno University in Kenya,shared their dream in a study group and an idea came up to have these dreams merged to achieve greater impact. Our aim is to change the African society future through empowerement and mentorship of children particularly those from poor backgrounds. The dreams merged and a goal was set to have the African child no longer under the yoke of ignorance of what constantly has become part of their lives.

She Rises

She rises program was developed to motivate the girl child in the schools. This was after a motivation charity drive at a school in the area around the University.This led to the realisation that the form 4 class had a huge number of boys compared to the girl child.
We were informed that girls dropped out due to:

  • lack of sanitary towels
  • early pregnancies
  • early marriages

VOLUNTEER WITH US.

Volunteership programs is a mentorship programs where the volunteers undergo training sessions such as Digitales and The Rises programs.The trainings are meant to empower and equip the volunteers with skills and techniques required for the projects of the organisation. The mentorship sessions are a highly effective method for volunteer development to ensure positive impacts.

The volunteer mentorships are conducted through one on one sessions between the volunteers and members of the organisation. They help them become better leaders and team players to allow effectiveness of the sessions.
